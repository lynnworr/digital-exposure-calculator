About

The Digital Exposure Calculator helps you estimate how much you’re exposed to online advertising, sponsored content, and tracking activity based on your last 7 days of internet use.

It’s designed to be simple, fast, and honest: results are shown as ranges instead of pretending the internet behaves the same for everyone.

Important: This site does not provide legal, medical, or financial advice. It’s informational only.

How it works

You enter time spent on social media, video platforms, and general browsing. The calculator multiplies those hours by conservative industry ranges (e.g., ads/hour and tracking events/hour). Your estimates will vary depending on your region, subscriptions (ad-free plans), device settings, and browsing habits.

Does this site store my data?

Your inputs are processed in your browser. If you click “Save this week,” the site stores your last saved summary locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage) so you can compare week-to-week.
